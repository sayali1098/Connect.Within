About Us

Connect.Within is a single destination for effective evidence based solution for better mental health. It is designed to help you build the strength to face life's ups and downs.

Our Story

Connect.Within was founded in Pune ,Maharashtra in 2018 along with a team of expert QCI certified trainers-- United with a belief that mindfulness can make people's lives better. Yoga, Meditation and Exercise has a significant impact on the lives of people, of what makes people thrive and lead meaningful lives __ so why don't more people know about it!

Our Mission

At Connect.within we are taking a 21st century approach to building a well-lived life. In a world that's increasingly stressful and complex, we want you to take control of your emotional life -and we aim to help you with that.
